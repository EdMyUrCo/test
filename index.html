What is pop it?

A Pop-It is a fidget toy consisting of a usually-brightly colored silicone tray with pokable bubbles, similar to bubble wrap, that can be flipped and re-used. They come in a variety of colors, shapes, and sizes, and even come in wearable formats.

Benefits

It is a 'fidget toy', that is, a toy to reduce stress and anxiety, widely used by minors who suffer from autism spectrum disorders or ADHD due to the benefits that its stimulation brings them. In addition, it can be applied for educational purposes, such as the reinforcement of different subjects.
